# About

## Vision

Rooch will drive the trend of Web3 boom and will be the infra for the next 100M user killer DApp.
For that we need to spend effort on two things:

- A product that inspire innovations and solves practical pain points for developers
- A community who are both users and builders of the Rooch project

## Rooch DAO

Rooch DAO is the organisation contributing to the Rooch project.
The detailed guideline of Rooch DAO is to be published.
For now, if you would like to participate in the Rooch project, feel free to join the [Rooch Discord](https://discord.gg/rooch) and let us know.
